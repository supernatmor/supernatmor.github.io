About Me


Since childhood I have always held a passion for electronics and technology. In college I minored in Software and Information Systems and my first job out of college was working as a cell phone repair technician for a 3rd party wireless retailer. I also developed a passion for creating things while apprenticing for a cabinet maker. It is my goal to find a career where I can combine my love of technology with the satisfaction of creating a product from scratch.

My interests outside of work range from cooking and video games to hiking and snowboarding. It is my dream to relocate to Colorado where I can spend more of my time in the mountains and pursuing my interests and hobbies.
